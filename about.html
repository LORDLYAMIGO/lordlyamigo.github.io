About

This space is a personal archive of language, thought, and design fragments. It is built to hold contradictions: contemplation and ambition, tradition and experiment, quietness and color.

The visual style stays intentionally hand-made: paper textures, outlined frames, and playful blocks. The writing follows the same rule and favors honest questions over polished certainty.

Everything here is still evolving. This page is a living note, not a fixed biography.

Built with care, noise, and curiosity.
